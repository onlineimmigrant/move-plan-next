## Three-Tier AI Architecture

Coded Harmony's AI system is built on a sophisticated three-tier architecture that balances power, flexibility, and control. This design ensures that the right people have access to the right AI capabilities while maintaining security and governance.

### Tier 1: System Models (Superadmin Level)

**Global AI Intelligence Available to All**

At the foundation are **System Models**—powerful, pre-configured AI agents managed by platform superadmins. These are production-ready models that organizations can enable instantly.

**Key Features:**
- **28+ Pre-Configured Models**: Including GPT-4, Claude, Grok, Gemini, DeepSeek, and more
- **Organization Targeting**: Models filtered by organization type (legal, education, immigration, general)
- **Pricing Plan Gating**: Access controlled by subscription tier (free, starter, pro, enterprise)
- **Token Management**: Built-in usage limits and quota enforcement
- **Trial Access**: Test premium models before committing
- **Task Definitions**: Pre-built workflows for common business tasks

**Example System Models:**
- **Content Writer AI**: Generates blog posts, articles, and web copy
- **Support Agent AI**: Answers customer questions with brand-specific context
- **Email Marketer AI**: Crafts compelling email campaigns
- **Legal Document AI**: Assists with contract review and generation (for law firms)
- **Course Creator AI**: Develops educational content and lesson plans (for schools)

**Benefits:**
- ✅ No configuration required—ready to use immediately
- ✅ Professionally tuned for optimal performance
- ✅ Regular updates with latest AI capabilities
- ✅ Cost-effective access to multiple AI providers
- ✅ Secure and compliant with data regulations

### Tier 2: Organization Models (Admin Level)

**Customized AI for Your Business**

Organization admins can customize AI capabilities to match their specific needs, creating a middle layer between global models and individual users.

**Key Features:**
- **Enable/Disable System Models**: Control which AI models your team can access
- **Custom AI Agents**: Create organization-specific AI with custom instructions
- **User Quotas**: Set token limits per user or team
- **Brand Voice Configuration**: Train AI to match your communication style
- **Workflow Automation**: Design AI-powered business processes
- **Access Control**: Determine which roles can use which AI models

**Example Use Cases:**

**Law Firm:**
- Create a "Client Intake AI" that understands your specific practice areas
- Configure legal research AI with approved citation formats
- Set strict compliance rules for document generation
- Limit usage of experimental models to senior partners

**Educational Institution:**
- Enable only education-focused AI models
- Create custom "Assignment Grader AI" with your rubric
- Configure AI tutors for different subjects
- Set higher token limits for teachers, lower for students

**E-commerce Business:**
- Create "Product Description AI" trained on your brand voice
- Enable customer service AI with access to your knowledge base
- Configure marketing AI with your campaign templates
- Set seasonal usage limits aligned with sales cycles

**Benefits:**
- ✅ Align AI capabilities with organizational needs
- ✅ Maintain brand consistency across AI-generated content
- ✅ Control costs with user-level quotas
- ✅ Ensure compliance with industry regulations
- ✅ Scale AI adoption gradually across your team

**Key Features:**

### How the Three Tiers Work Together

The architecture creates a powerful hierarchy of AI intelligence:

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  Tier 1: SYSTEM MODELS (28+ AI Models)     │
│  Managed by: Superadmin                     │
│  Scope: Global, all organizations           │
│  Examples: GPT-4, Claude, Grok, Gemini      │
└──────────────────┬──────────────────────────┘
                   │ Organizations enable/configure
                   ▼
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  Tier 2: ORGANIZATION MODELS                │
│  Managed by: Admin                          │
│  Scope: Single organization                 │
│  Examples: Custom brand AI, workflow agents │
└──────────────────┬──────────────────────────┘
                   │ Users access and customize
                   ▼
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  Tier 3: USER MODELS                        │
│  Managed by: Individual users               │
│  Scope: Personal workspace                  │
│  Examples: Personal assistants, favorites   │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

**Access Flow Example:**

1. **Superadmin** adds "GPT-4 Content Writer" to system models
2. **Law Firm Admin** enables it for their organization with legal-specific instructions
3. **Attorney** uses it to draft client communications while adding personal case context
4. **Result**: Powerful AI + organizational compliance + personal efficiency

**Governance and Control:**

- **Superadmin** controls which models exist globally and their base capabilities
- **Admin** controls which models their organization uses and how
- **User** controls when and how they use available models
- **System** enforces token limits, tracks usage, and maintains audit logs

### Token Management and Fair Usage

To ensure fair access and cost control, Coded Harmony implements sophisticated token management:

**Token Tracking:**
- Every AI interaction consumes tokens (roughly 1 token = 0.75 words)
- Real-time usage monitoring at user, organization, and system levels
- Historical usage data for budgeting and planning

**Quota Enforcement:**
- Daily, weekly, or monthly limits per model
- Organization-level budgets allocated across users
- Automatic resets and rollover options
- Grace periods for accidental overages

**Pricing Tiers:**
- **Free Tier**: Basic models with generous limits for small teams
- **Starter Tier**: More models and higher quotas for growing businesses
- **Pro Tier**: Advanced models and priority access
- **Enterprise Tier**: Unlimited usage and dedicated support
